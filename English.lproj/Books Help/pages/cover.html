Cover Image

The cover image view is in the lower-left portion of the main window.

A cover will appear when you select a single book. When no or multiple books are selected, the display will be grayed out.

Change the display size

You can change the size of the cover view by dragging both the horizontal split-pane control in the left side of the window to increase the height, and by dragging the vertical split-pane control that separates the book lists from the books. Larger covers will be resized to fit within the available space without distorting the cover's proportions.
